About Hands & Free Health Campaign

We believe that medical treatment is basic right of and human being. Unfortunately, there are poor, needy and financially insolvent people who are struggling get proper adequate medical treatment. They do not even have the adequate money to go to a doctor for medical treatment, buy medicines and do proper medical investigations. For these reasons we HANDS, organize free medical campaign. In this campaign, we appoint a team of skilled doctors who provides medical treatments to poor, needy and financially insolvent people totally free of costs even we provide free medicines and other required medical investigations totally free of charge.

With the free medical campaign, we have helped many people to achieve necessary medical care and with this campaign we always encourage and spread awareness between the mass people to be cautious about their health and safety.

HANDS organize such campaigns once every month. However, for now the campaign is only available at the headquarter of HANDS and we are hopeful to launch this type of campaign in all other branches in near future.
