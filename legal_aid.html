Legal Aid

HANDS provide legal advice, counseling services and represent all levels of justice system through legal assistance of all means, especially for certain people who are unable to afford legal representation and access the justice. Legal services such as Mediation, Litigation, Awareness/Training, PIL and Communication are offered by HANDS.

Mediation

HANDS resolves Petty Criminal, Family, Land, Financial, Dowry related dispute through mediation, which is a method of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) mechanism.

Litigation

ANDS provide Public interest litigation (PIL) services by filling petitions in the Supreme Court of Bangladesh. We conduct PIL in collaboration with other concerned organization and individuals.
